Violence

3 Rings within. Violence against neighbors, themselves, and the church.

Neighbors

Sin

Violence against neighbors.

Punishment

Made to boil in blood, and shot by arrows if they get out higher than they are deemed worthy.

Poetic Justice

Those torturedmust boil in the blood they creted in life by violence.

People

Alexander the great, who was violent against many of his countrymen as a tyrant.

Themselves

Sin

Violence against themselves. (Suicide)

Punishment

Turned into trees and are immobile. Tortured and pecked at by Harpies.

Poetic Justice

Never be returned to their bodies because they didn't properly appreciate them in the first place.

People

Pier della Vigna, who felt so ashamed by the lies of shcemers, took his own life.

The Church

Sin

For those violent against God.

Punishment

Three circles:
Blasphamers - Just on hot sand
Sodomites - Also rained upon my fire
Violent against art - also with purses around thier necks

People

Capaneus, Besieged Thebes. He is very defiant, still, and says hell shall never break him.
