Gluttony

The gluttonous wallow in a vile, putrid slush produced by a ceaseless, foul, icy rain – "a great storm of putrefaction" – as punishment for subjecting their reason to a voracious appetite.

Gluttony

Sin

Gluttonous.

Punishment

They are rained upon by skinky rain and garbage, and stand in worms decomposing the mess.

Poetic Justice

over-indulgence is grotesque, ugly, and causes waste, in which the sinners must eternally live in. Constantly barked at by Cerberus.
